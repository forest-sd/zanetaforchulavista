Welcome

Thank you for visiting Zaneta for Chula Vista. Our campaign is built on the belief that effective leadership starts with listening — to residents, business owners, community organizations, and the diverse voices that make Chula Vista one of California's most vibrant cities. We're committed to transparent governance, responsible growth, and ensuring that every neighborhood shares in Chula Vista's success.

Community First

Policies driven by community input, not special interests. Town halls, neighborhood meetings, and open-door accessibility.

Smart Growth

Responsible development that creates jobs, preserves neighborhood character, and invests in infrastructure before growth outpaces it.

Public Safety

Supporting first responders with the resources they need while investing in prevention programs that make communities safer at the root.
