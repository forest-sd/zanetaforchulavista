Our Platform

Real solutions for Chula Vista's real challenges.

Housing Affordability

Expanding housing options through smart zoning, incentivizing affordable development, protecting tenants from displacement, and streamlining permitting for ADUs and infill projects.

Infrastructure Investment

Fixing roads, upgrading water systems, expanding parks, and building the infrastructure needed to support Chula Vista's growth without sacrificing quality of life.

Economic Development

Attracting quality employers, supporting small businesses, investing in workforce development, and ensuring that economic growth creates opportunities for Chula Vista residents.

Parks & Recreation

Expanding green space, upgrading existing parks, building new community centers, and ensuring every neighborhood has access to quality recreational facilities and programs.
