About Zaneta

A lifetime of service. A vision for Chula Vista's future.

Who I Am

I'm a Chula Vista resident, community leader, and lifelong advocate for the people and neighborhoods that make our city great. My career in public service has been driven by a simple principle: government works best when it works for everyone. I've spent years building relationships across our diverse communities, listening to concerns, and turning those conversations into action. I'm running because I believe Chula Vista deserves leadership that's accessible, accountable, and focused on the issues that matter most to working families.
